Academic & Research Projects

This page presents selected academic and practical projects demonstrating alignment with research interests in Graph Machine Learning, Trustworthy AI, Safe Machine Learning, and Data-Centric AI Systems.

Selected Projects

    Related courses: CT-4222 (Digital Image Processing & Pattern Recognition), CT-3231 (Design and Analysis of Algorithms)
    Developed a pattern recognition pipeline incorporating feature extraction, graph-based representations, and algorithmic optimization techniques. This project reflects foundational skills for graph neural networks and data-efficient learning.

  2. Simulation and Modeling of Complex Networked Systems
    Related course: CT-4211 (Simulation and Modeling)
    Modeled and simulated complex systems with interacting entities to evaluate system behavior under varying conditions, supporting research themes in robustness, distribution shifts, and safe machine learning.
